Hello! To use this web-app, all you need to do is select the chord you want to play and the specifications of your guitar! It's easy as that. So, what does this program do? Well, it shows you the practical fingerings of the chord you want to play! If you hit "Next Fingering," it will show you a fingering further up the fret board! The places you put your fingers will light up on the fret board.
